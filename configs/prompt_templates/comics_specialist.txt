ADDITIONAL CONTEXT FOR COMICS:
- Read panels in order: left-to-right, top-to-bottom (Western convention)
- EXCEPTION: Manga (Japanese comics) reads right-to-left
- Speech bubbles belong to the character nearest their tail/pointer
- Sound effects are large stylized text (e.g., "POW", "CRASH", "BOOM")
- Narrator boxes are usually rectangular with a different background
- Thought bubbles are usually cloud-shaped (vs. speech bubbles which are oval)
- Character names are often shown in bold within dialogue
- Action lines around a figure indicate movement or emphasis
