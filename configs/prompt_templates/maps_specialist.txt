ADDITIONAL CONTEXT FOR MAPS:
- Road types and names are visually encoded (line styles, colors)
- Grid coordinates are alphanumeric (e.g., "E-10") — always uppercase the letter
- Mileage is shown on road segments as small numbers between cities
- Legend is typically in the corner — consult it for symbol meanings
- Scale bars define distances: use them when answering distance questions
- Compass rose indicates orientation (usually North at top)
- Place names are in larger font than road labels
